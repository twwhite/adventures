Q: What is there to do in the area near the venue?

A: There’s tons to do! The venue is sandwiched between the east side of the Olympic Peninsula and the Puget Sound.

There is tons of green-space to explore, like Olympic National Park, Olympic National Forest, the Hoh Rainforest, the Quinault Rainforest, and more.

Seattle also offers a cityscape with an aquarium, the Space Needle, and dozens of other attractions. There is a ferry that crosses the sound that could be a fun day-trip. Olympia and Tacoma are also nearby and offer their own quaint city vibes.

If you head north of the venue, you can reach some cool port towns like Port Townsend or Port Angeles. This area is also great for wildlife boat tours in the sound and around the San Juan Islands.

There is so much that we haven’t had the chance to do yet up here, but let us know if you want recommendations!
