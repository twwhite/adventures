On-Site Tent Camping

We booked a Luxury (tents), Rustic (outside), and Complimentary (includes bathrooms) group campsite experience for our VIP guests (everyone!).

The campsite is an easy, short walk from the venue.

We encourage anyone who likes to camp to join in on the fun. Susie and Tim will be camping both Thurs. and Fri. nights. It’s BYO-everything, but we can probably continue to provide refreshments throughout the night!

There is a permanent open-faced rooved shelter with exposed bed-bunks on site for those who only want to carry a sleeping bag through TSA.

For you brave souls joining us, get in touch if you have specific questions or if you forsee needing to borrow some camping supplies! :)

We will be camping on-site for a few nights surrounding, but not including the night-of the wedding.
